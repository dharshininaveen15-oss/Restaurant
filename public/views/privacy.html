Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

We collect information necessary to provide a seamless "Guest Mode" experience. This includes:

  • Order Data: Items selected, quantities, and special instructions.
  • Device Info: Browser type and IP address for fraud prevention.
  • Session Data: Temporary identifiers to link your table to your cart.

2. Data Security & Encryption

We implement industry-standard SSL encryption for all data transmitted between your device and our servers. Order histories are stored in secured MongoDB instances with restricted access.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data. However, we may share necessary details with:

  • Payment Processors: To handle secure transactions.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our menu to improve UI/UX.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

QuickServe uses Local Storage to maintain your cart state. Unlike traditional cookies, this data is stored locally on your device and is not used for cross-site tracking or advertising.

Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your order history from our local logs. Contact privacy@quickserve.io for assistance.
